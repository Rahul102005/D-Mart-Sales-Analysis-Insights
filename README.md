D-Mart Sales Analysis & Business Insights
Project Overview
This project analyzes 4200+ D-Mart retail transactions to generate business insights using Python, SQL, and Power BI. It demonstrates an end-to-end data analytics workflow including data cleaning, analysis, KPI generation, and dashboard creation.
Dataset Details
• Total Records: 4200+
• Time Period: 2024
• Multiple Store Locations
• Multiple Payment Modes
Data Cleaning & Feature Engineering (Python)
• Handled missing values
• Replaced null payment_mode with 'Unknown'
• Replaced null customer_type with 'Regular'
• Removed duplicate records
• Converted date column to datetime format
• Extracted month from date for time-based analysis
SQL Analysis
• Total Sales Calculation
• Sales by Store City
• Sales by Category
• Payment Mode Distribution
• Revenue by Customer Type
• Monthly Sales Trend
Key Business KPIs
• Total Sales: 47M
• Total Transactions: 4K
• Average Order Value: 11.19K
Power BI Dashboard Features
• Sales by Store City (Bar Chart)
• Sales by Category (Treemap)
• Sales by Payment Mode (Donut Chart)
• Time-based Sales Trend (Line Chart)
• Interactive Filters (Customer Type, Store City, Category, Payment Mode)
Business Insights
• Madurai generated the highest sales.
• Grocery & Staples contributed the highest revenue.
• Card payments were slightly higher than other modes.
• Regular customers generated more revenue than new customers.
• Sales remained stable throughout the year with minor fluctuations.
